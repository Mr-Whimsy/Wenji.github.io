About Me

I am a fourth-year undergraduate student at Southwest Jiaotong University && University of Leeds, advised by Xun Gong.My research interests lie in Computer Vision and Robotics.During 2022-2023, under the guidance of Professor Xun Gong, I participated in a national-level innovation and entrepreneurship project for college students. The project focused on the synchronization localization and mapping navigation problems of unmanned vehicles. I applied SLAM algorithms and utilized Google's Cartographer library for real-time localization and map generation in unfamiliar environments. From 2023-2024, under the guidance of Professor Xun Gong and PhD candidate Lin Fan, I conducted research on domain generalization in medical image classification and recognition. The purpose is to resolve domain differences between different medical data centers.
